## Button States

### Fetch Fleet Vehicles Button
- **Initial**: Enabled (blue)
- **Loading**: Disabled
- **After Success**: Enabled (can be clicked again to refresh)

### Sync Missing Vehicles Button
- **Initial**: Disabled (gray with lock icon)
- **After Fetch (with missing)**: Enabled (green)
- **During Sync**: Disabled
- **After Sync Success**: Disabled (no more vehicles to sync)
- **After Partial Fail**: May remain enabled if vehicles still need syncing

## Integration with Route Management

The Fleet Management feature is **independent** but **complementary** to the Route Management:

1. **Use Fleet Management FIRST** to populate your vehicle database
2. **Then use Route Management** to create reviews for specific routes
3. Routes will now find vehicles automatically (green checkmarks instead of red warnings)
